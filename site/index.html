The actual idea

The bridge between you and AI.

You have an image in your head. AI knows how to paint almost anything. The hard part — the part nobody sells you a course on, because it shouldn't need one — is in the middle.

VisualPrompt is that middle. You stage your idea like a tiny film set. The AI takes it from there.

  • You place the things.
  • You point the camera.
  • You say the vibe in plain words.
  • The AI handles colors, light, materials, mood.

How it works

Three small things. One real picture.

No tutorial. The whole flow takes about as long as writing a tweet.

  1. 01

    Place

    Drag simple blocks onto the floor. A box for the couch, a sphere for the cat, a cone for the lamp. Like building with toy bricks. You can't break it.

  2. 02

    Frame

    Spin around your scene until it looks like the picture you wanted. Closer? Lower? More tilted? Move the camera, not the words.

  3. 03

    Describe

    One sentence is enough. "Cosy reading nook, evening light, soft watercolor." No magic words, no negative prompts, no formula.
