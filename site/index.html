Spatial prompting for AI

Show the model where things belong—not only how they look.

VisualPrompt is a browser editor where you compose a 3D layout, camera, and scene context, then attach a natural-language prompt so generative pipelines can interpret space and framing—not guesses from prose alone.

Why layouts beat text-alone prompts

  • You place, models see framingPosition entities directly; the viewport encodes occlusion, scale cues, and camera intent.
  • ConsistencyReuse scene structure across iterations instead of rewriting spatial prose each time.
  • Fewer hallucinationsModels anchor on geometry and viewpoint where the toolchain supports structured scene snapshots.

The text-prompt bottleneck

Phrases like “a cube slightly above and behind a sphere” are ambiguous scale, ambiguous units, and easy to misunderstand. Humans and diffusion models alike interpret words differently—which shows up as wrong composition or inconsistent rerolls.

Use the scene itself as context

VisualPrompt captures your scene—placement, proportions, framing—and pairs it with a normal creative brief. Together that becomes the visual prompt: layout plus language, grounded in pixels and depth instead of shorthand.

How it works

Three coarse steps—from empty stage to handed-off generation intent.

  1. 1

    Compose in 3D

    Add entities from a palette, place them on the ground plane, tweak height with vertical drag, and orbit the camera the way you would in a nimble sandbox.

  2. 2

    Align the visual prompt

    Iterate until the framing matches intent: occlusion, horizons, silhouette reads, emphasis. The viewport is cheap to explore compared to speculative text.

  3. 3

    Combine with plain language

    Add mood, palette, realism level, constraints. The toolchain marries textual goals with grounded scene cues for rendering or video hops.

What ships in the product

Editor affordances you can reason about when helping users or extending the stack.

Canvas & orbit camera
Manipulate viewpoint with familiar orbit controls plus keyboard zoom so creative direction maps directly to renders.
Entity lifecycle
Sidebar spawn, grounded placement with optional snap, trash affordance—all optimized for tactile iteration.
Post & lighting tools
Integrated color correction knobs for rapid look exploration before export snapshots.
Integrated generation
Backed by Supabase and secure edge functions—images build from scene context plus authored prompts rather than pasted URLs.
